ABOUT US

Dimamzo's Kitchen believes that simple tastes better. Always has, always will. The cuisines we offer brings the township together. There are few things in everyday life that are better than spending time with friends and family while eating the best South African cuisines. Nothing makes us more joyful than knowing we are playing a minor role in helping you, our customer, create these kind of experiences.
